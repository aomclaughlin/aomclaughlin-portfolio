CDC Tobacco Website Redesign for the Office on Smoking and Health

The CDC Tobacco website had grown into a comprehensive but content-heavy resource. As Content Strategist, I led modernization efforts to improve clarity, accessibility, and equity framing.

Read More

Problem

  • Navigation reflected years of additions rather than a unified structure.
  • Content length and density made scanning difficult for non-experts.
  • Accessibility standards had evolved since the last redesign.
  • Health equity and disparities content was not highlighted in line with CDC’s modern framing.

Solution

  • Streamlined navigation aligned to audience needs (public, providers, public health).
  • Content audit and consolidation to reduce redundancy and simplify pathways.
  • Visual storytelling and improved readability, with whitespace, infographics, and feature stories.
  • Accessibility compliance updates, aligning with WCAG 2.1 standards.
  • Equity-first presentation, elevating disparities and health equity content.
